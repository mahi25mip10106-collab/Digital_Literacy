Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

 Do’s
- Think before posting anything online  
- Maintain a respectful and positive tone  
- Protect your personal information  
- Use strong privacy settings  
- Share meaningful and appropriate content  

 Don’ts
- Do not share passwords or sensitive data  
- Avoid posting offensive or harmful content  
- Do not spread fake news or misinformation  
- Avoid oversharing personal life details  
- Do not engage in online arguments or negativity
