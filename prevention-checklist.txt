Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist
Account & Password Security
- Use strong passwords with a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols (minimum 8–12 characters)  
- Never reuse the same password across multiple accounts  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for email, social media, and banking apps  
- Log out from shared or public devices after use  

 Safe Browsing & Downloads
- Always check the website URL (look for https:// and correct spelling) before entering personal information  
- Avoid clicking on unknown links from emails, SMS, or social media messages  
- Download apps and software only from official sources (Google Play Store, App Store, official websites)  
- Do not open suspicious attachments, even if they appear from known contacts  

 Device & Network Safety
- Keep your phone, laptop, and apps updated with the latest security patches  
- Install antivirus software and scan your device regularly  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities like banking or logging into accounts  
- Use a secure lock (PIN, fingerprint, or face lock) on your devices  

 UPI & Financial Safety (Very Important)
- Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, CVV, or bank details with anyone — no bank ever asks for this  
- Do not approve “Collect Requests” unless you initiated the transaction yourself  
- Always verify the receiver’s name before sending money  
- Remember: To receive money, you DO NOT need to enter your UPI PIN  

Social Media Awareness
- Do not share personal information like address, phone number, or college details publicly  
- Think before posting — once shared online, content can be difficult to remove  
- Avoid engaging in online arguments or responding to unknown profiles  
- Report and block suspicious or abusive accounts immediately  

 Reporting Cybercrime (India)
- Report any cybercrime immediately at: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930  
- Inform your bank immediately in case of financial fraud to prevent further loss
